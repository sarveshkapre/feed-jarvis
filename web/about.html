Feed Jarvis Studio
Human tone, repeatable process.
Studio About GitHub

Designed for editorial and social teams.

Feed Jarvis turns recurring updates into confident, on-brand drafts without forcing you into a heavy workflow. It keeps everything local, gives you repeatable personas, and leaves the final voice in your hands.

Why it exists

Product updates, release notes, and company news already live in feeds. Feed Jarvis bridges the gap between those feeds and a thoughtful social calendar so your team can stay consistent without starting from scratch every time.

Principles

Quiet UI, bold typography, and a clear three-step flow.

Local-first by default. You can run it on your machine without sending data elsewhere.

Multi-channel drafts (X, LinkedIn, newsletters) that you can edit and ship quickly.

Who it serves

Social managers, founders, and newsroom teams who need to publish quickly but still sound like themselves. Feed Jarvis is the bridge between incoming updates and finished posts.
